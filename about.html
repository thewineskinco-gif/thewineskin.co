Where Every Stitch Tells a Story

Discover the passion and craftsmanship behind every Wineskin piece

"We don't just make leather goods.
We create companions for life's journey."

Wineskin was born from a simple belief: that the objects we carry daily should be as meaningful as the moments they witness. Founded in 2010 by a collective of master craftsmen, we set out to revive the lost art of slow leatherwork in an age of mass production.

Each piece begins with the careful selection of full-grain hides from responsible tanneries. We use only vegetable-tanned leather, allowing nature's timeline to create depth and character that synthetic processes cannot replicate.

Our artisans, many with decades of experience, hand-cut, stitch, and finish every item. A single bag can take up to 40 hours to complete. This patience ensures that when a Wineskin piece reaches you, it's ready to begin its story alongside yours.
